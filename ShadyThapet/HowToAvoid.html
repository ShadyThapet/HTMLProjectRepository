Follow This steps to avoid covid-19

  1. Wash your hands often.
  2. Use soap and water or an alcohol solution to sterilize your hands.
  3. Keep a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.
  4. Wear a muzzle when physical distancing is not possible.
  5. Do not touch your eyes, nose, or mouth.
  6. Make sure to cover your nose and mouth with your elbow or tissue when coughing or sneezing.
